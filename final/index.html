Mountain Family Candle Co

Mountain Family Candle Co is a homegrown business, run by Husband and Wife duo, Jasmine and Justin Brown. With the help of their niece and nephews, their goal is to create a business that is in every home.

Mountain Family Candle Co cares about sustainability and organic options for home decor. Using only soy wax and beeswax for their candles, quality is never in question.

Along with organic ingredients, Mountain Family Candle Co is dedicated to making candles with distinguished scent profiles that are sure to light up every room.

Mountain Family Candle Co makes candles, wax melts, and sells other home decor gifts for any home. Most of a person's life is spent inside the home. Having these materials make a home warm and full, and make the memories and moments more sweet.
By creating a home grown business, we keep family in mind.
