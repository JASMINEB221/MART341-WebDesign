Brownie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C butter
  • 1 C sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 C unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 C all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

      Directions

      1. Preheat oven to 350o (degrees Farenheight) and grease an 8" (inch) pan
      2. In large saucepan, melt butter, remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Beat cocoa powder, flour, salt and baking powder. Pour into pan.
      3. Bake for 25-30 minutes
